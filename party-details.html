Event Description

This distinguished celebration is crafted to provide an elegant, memorable experience with entertainment, engaging activities, ceremonial cake-cutting, professional photography, and delightful dining.

Your presence will make this milestone truly unforgettable.



Core / Guaranteed Benefits:

  • Guaranteed seating for you and your companions.
  • Full program access to all scheduled activities, entertainment, and ceremonies.
  • Dining & refreshments: multi-course meal or buffet, appetizers, desserts, and beverages.
  • Special dietary accommodations available.
  • Cake-cutting ceremony participation.
  • Official photo opportunities and group photographs.
  • Event program handout.
  • Party favors / souvenirs for all registered guests.

Designs / Styling Included:

  • Elegant table settings, centerpieces, and table décor.
  • Themed decoration throughout the venue according to chosen event theme.
  • Coordinated color schemes, linens, and floral arrangements.
  • Decorative lighting and backdrops for photo opportunities.
  • Special display areas for cake, gifts, or guestbook.
  • Optional custom décor or branding elements for VIP guests or reserved tables.

Optional / Premium Extras:

  • VIP / priority seating areas.
  • Special surprises, live performances, or interactive entertainment.
  • Customized guest recognition or acknowledgment during the program.
  • Reserved parking (if available).
  • Exclusive networking opportunities with special guests or hosts.
  • Refreshment / beverage stations for premium access.
  • Event photography prints or digital keepsakes.
  • Special gift packages or commemorative items.
  • Priority RSVP confirmation.
  • Special food & drink upgrades: dessert tables, specialty cakes, mocktails, cocktails, or themed food stations.

Important Notes:

  • Guests are kindly requested to observe proper decorum throughout the event.
  • Any damages to property, equipment, or materials belonging to the venue or hosts caused during the event will incur additional charges, which will be the responsibility of the reserving party.
  • Gifts are optional but appreciated.
